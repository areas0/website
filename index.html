The project

Bartendu is a cooking simulation game made with Unity3D 2019.3 in C#. It features notably an online multiplayer mode, various recipes and gameplay possibilites. A story mode is also available.

The context

This game has been made by EPITA students during 2nd semester. You will find here every document made during the project in addition to the game and credits.
